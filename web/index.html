Home

Streamline parking management with a simple platform that records check-in and check-out times, license plates, and vehicle-based rates, automatically calculating the total to pay.

The dashboard shows real-time active vehicles, movement history, and quick reports to keep daily operations transparent and under control.

  • Automatic entry and exit logging with precise timestamps.
  • License plate identification and vehicle-type classification.
  • Instant price calculation based on configured rates.
  • Reports for daily tracking and shift closing.

Reliability, speed, and control in one place, designed for owners of informal parking lots who need hassle-free management.

Services

Comprehensive tools to manage the parking lot from any device, with automated processes and clear data for decision-making.

Each service is designed to save time, prevent manual errors, and improve the payment experience for owners and drivers.

  • Access control: record vehicle check-in and check-out times.
  • Rate management: configure by vehicle type and time bands.
  • License plate lookup: quick search and history verification.

  • Reporting and audit: summaries of movements, revenue, and occupancy.

Close support and updates tailored to daily parking operations, without complex processes.
